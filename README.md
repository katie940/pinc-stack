# The Pincus Stack

Claude skills inspired by Mark Pincus's **"Life at the Speed of Play"** — the product-building frameworks that powered Zynga and shaped a generation of consumer products.

Think of this as a product founder's AI toolkit. Each skill channels a specific framework from Mark's book into a practical, interactive tool you can use with Claude.

## The Skills

### `/pbn` — Proven Better New Analysis
The core framework. Break down any product idea into what's **= Proven** (copy what has heat), **> Better** (improvements where 10/10 users say yes), and **+ New** (risky novel bets isolated as shots on goal). Includes the wildcat drilling matrix for ranking your New bets.

### `/boldbeats` — Bold Beat Ideation
Brainstorm high-impact features that create OMFG moments. Each idea is scored against Mark's bar: 25%+ of users try it, moves a key metric 10%+, quick to build and kill. Draws from the Golden Mechanics library (streaks, mystery crates, gifting, crew mechanics, and more).

### `/mlp` — Maximum Launch Product Pipeline
Kill the MVP. Design your **Minimum Idea State** (smallest test of the idea itself) leading to **Maximum Launch Product** (launch big with conviction). Includes ripomatic thinking, the "humanize before you productize" step, and kill criteria for failing fast.

### `/bluesheet` — Product Roadmapping OS
Build a roadmap in Mark's Blue Sheet format — the operating system that ran Zynga. Tracks features, expected outcomes, actual outcomes, engineering day estimates (with Rule of Four baked in), and OKR alignment. Includes a mouse nuts detector.

### `/deconstruct` — Competitive Teardown
AI-powered product analysis that breaks down any product pixel by pixel, mechanic by mechanic. Maps the core loop, retention mechanics, growth engine, monetization model, Golden Mechanics inventory, and heat map. Identifies what's transferable to your own product.

### `/markpincus` — The Meta-Advisor
Describe any product situation and get Mark's perspective channeled through every framework in the book. Knows when to invoke PBN vs. Bold Beats vs. "f*** scale, are you even right yet?" The one skill that ties all the others together.
