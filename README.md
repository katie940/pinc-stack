# The Pincus Stack

Claude skills inspired by [Mark Pincus](https://x.com/markpinc)' upcoming book, **"[Life at the Speed of Play](https://www.lifeatthespeedofplay.com/)"** (Harpercollins) — an unconventional, hands-on guide to turning ideas into products that matter—and that people love from the legendary Silicon Valley pioneer, founder of Zynga, and creator of Stanford Graduate School of Business’s product management course.

This is any product maker's AI toolkit. Each skill channels a specific framework from Mark's book into a practical, interactive tool you can use with Claude.

Who this is for:
* founders, makers who want to build products people can't live without
* first-time Claude Code users who want structured processes and frameworks instead of a blank prompt

## The Skills

### `/pbn` — Proven Better New Analysis
Mark's core framework for product innovation. Break down any product idea into what's **= Proven** (copy what has heat), **> Better** (improvements where 10/10 users say yes), and **+ New** (novel bets that are inherently risky, as they're unproven, so you'll want to isolate them). Includes the "wildcat drilling" matrix to help you prioritize and rank your New ideas.

### `/boldbeats` — Bold Beat Ideation
Brainstorm high-impact features that create OMFG moments. Each idea is scored against a hypothetical imagining of the bar Mark used at Zynga: 25%+ of users try it, moves a key metric 10%+, quick to build and kill. Draws from the Golden Mechanics library -- features that are based in psychology, that are often used in gaming, like streaks, mystery crates, gifting, crew mechanics, and more.

### `/mlp` — Maximum Launch Product Pipeline
Burn the MVP. Instead, build a **Minimum Idea State** (the atomic unit of the idea itself)so you can test a bunch of ideas quickly. Then, once you have conviction, build your **Maximum Launch Product** (a product you have conviction about). Includes Zynga terminology & thinking: ripomatics (prototypes, inspired by Hollywood's same-named 'sizzle reels'), Brian Chesky's product-building approach that Mark has called "humanize before you productize", and criteria for failing fast.

### `/bluesheet` — Product Roadmapping OS
Build a roadmap in Blue Sheet format — the operating system that ran Zynga. Tracks features, expected outcomes, actual outcomes, engineering day estimates (with Rule of Four baked in), and OKR alignment. Includes a mouse nuts detector (tiny, insignificant changes that don’t move the needle).

### `/deconstruct` — Competitive Teardown
AI-powered product analysis that breaks down any product pixel by pixel, feature by feature. Maps the core loop, retention mechanics, growth engine, monetization model, Golden Mechanics inventory, and heat map. Finally, it identifies what's transferable to your own product.

### `/markpincus` — The Meta-Advisor
Describe any product situation and get Mark's perspective channeled through every framework in the book. Knows when to invoke PBN vs. Bold Beats, and when to push back on you to take a hill and get to conviction. **EXPERIMENTAL**
